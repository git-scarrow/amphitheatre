## Records requested

**1. Storm-sewer GIS / infrastructure data for the downtown area around the site**

- Digital GIS files (shapefile, geodatabase, or exported PDF/DXF) showing storm-sewer infrastructure within approximately 1,000 feet of the site, including:
  - Catch basin / inlet locations
  - Manhole locations and rim/invert elevations (where available)
  - Pipe sizes, materials, and flow directions
  - Outfall locations to Little Traverse Bay and/or the Bear River served by this system
  - Any known pipe inverts or as-built elevations

**2. Construction as-built drawings**

- Any as-built or record drawings for storm-sewer construction within or adjacent to the blocks bounded approximately by Bay Street (north), Howard Street (west), Michigan Street (south), and Division Street (east).

**3. Drainage area maps or basin delineations**

- Any engineering drawings, drainage studies, or GIS layers identifying the contributing drainage area to the storm system serving the site and surrounding downtown blocks.

**4. Drainage and stormwater records for the excavation itself**

- Any records addressing stormwater, ponding, drainage, or dewatering at the former Petoskey Pointe excavation ("the pit"), including: approved site plans or grading/drainage plans from the Petoskey Pointe development (approximately 2005–2009); dewatering permits or discharge authorizations during excavation; any development-agreement provisions concerning stormwater; and any post-2007 correspondence or studies concerning drainage or standing water at the site.

**5. MS4 outfall inventory for the downtown system**

- Any MS4 outfall location records or NPDES-permitted discharge points for the storm system serving the downtown blocks, at Little Traverse Bay or the Bear River.
