---

## Records requested

**1. Storm-sewer GIS / infrastructure data for the Bayfront Park area**

- Digital GIS files (shapefile, geodatabase, or exported PDF/DXF) showing storm-sewer infrastructure within approximately 2,000 feet of Bayfront Park, including:
  - Catch basin / inlet locations
  - Manhole locations and rim/invert elevations (where available)
  - Pipe sizes, materials, and flow directions
  - Outfall locations to Little Traverse Bay and/or the Bear River
  - Any known pipe inverts or as-built elevations

**2. Construction as-built drawings**

- Any as-built or record drawings for storm-sewer construction within or adjacent to Bayfront Park, including the area bounded approximately by West Lake Street (north), Howard Street (west), Petoskey Street (south), and the bay shoreline (north/east).

**3. Drainage area maps or basin delineations**

- Any engineering drawings, drainage studies, or GIS layers identifying the contributing drainage area to the storm system serving Bayfront Park or the surrounding blocks.

**4. Bayfront Park drainage study or stormwater management report**

- Any existing engineering reports, stormwater studies, or master-drainage analyses that address Bayfront Park or the "pit" depression in the west portion of the park.

**5. NPDES/MS4 outfall inventory near Bayfront Park**

- Any MS4 outfall location records or NPDES-permitted discharge points within the park or at the bay shoreline adjacent to the park.
