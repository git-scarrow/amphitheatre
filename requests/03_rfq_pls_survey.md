We are preparing a planning-grade design study for an outdoor amphitheater and civic event garden at **Bayfront Park, City of Petoskey, Emmet County, Michigan**, on the parcel(s) immediately west of the intersection of Petoskey Street and Howard Street, adjacent to the Bear River mouth and Little Traverse Bay.

We are soliciting qualifications and a scope/fee proposal for the following survey services, which we intend to combine into a single mobilization.

---

## Scope of services requested

### B-1 — Boundary & ALTA-type survey

- Establish and mark all parcel boundaries and right-of-way lines bounding the park, with particular attention to:
  - East boundary adjacent to Petoskey Street
  - South boundary adjacent to Howard Street
  - Northwest boundary / bay shoreline proximity
- Identify and locate all easements, encumbrances, and utilities of record (coordinate with title/Register of Deeds research).
- Determine whether the ~589-ft-elevation depression in the northwest corner of the park and the proposed northeast outfall route fall on-parcel or require easement.
- Deliverable: ALTA/NSPS Land Title Survey (or equivalent boundary certificate) and legal description, in digital (DXF/PDF/shapefile) and print format.

### B-2 — Design-grade topographic survey

- Ground survey with conventional shots and breaklines sufficient for a **1-ft contour interval** design-control surface across the entire park (approximately 4–6 acres), with higher density in the central bowl and amphitheater footprint (approximately 2 acres).
- Tie-in horizontal control to NAD83(2011) Michigan Central (EPSG:6494, international feet); vertical control to **NAVD88** using benchmarks tied to NGS control.
- Locate all surface features: trees (6-in caliper and larger, with species where feasible), existing paths, structures, utilities (exposed), curbs, light poles, and any visible drainage infrastructure (inlets, manholes, culvert ends).
- Deliverable: Digital terrain model (DTM/TIN), 1-ft contour DXF, and coordinate list; control summary and benchmark documentation.

### B-3 — OHWM and shoreline survey

- Locate and document the **Ordinary High Water Mark (OHWM)** of Little Traverse Bay and the Bear River in the vicinity of the park's north/northwest shoreline.
- Establish the OHWM elevation in NAVD88 to confirm or correct the current planning assumption (IGLD85 ≈ 581 ft ± seasonal range).
- Locate any existing outfall or shoreline drainage structures within approximately 500 ft of the park's northwest corner.
- Deliverable: OHWM delineation map and elevation documentation for EGLE Part 301/325 assessment.

---

## Project context (for scope planning)

- Site area: approximately 4–6 acres total park; ~2-acre primary amphitheater footprint.
- Existing data: 2015 USGS LiDAR at 1-m resolution (±0.5–1 ft vertical), EPSG:6494. Available for pre-survey planning.
- Known relief: bowl floor ~609 ft NAVD88, rim ~618 ft, maximum relief ~9 ft within the closed depression; overall park range ~580–625 ft.
- Industrial/fill history: the depression may be partially filled; locate and flag any visible surface evidence of fill or debris.
