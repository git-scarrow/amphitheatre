## Records requested

**1. Current parcel identification and ownership**

- Parcel identification number(s) (PIN) for the parcel(s) comprising Bayfront Park, including any parcels abutting the park's western edge at or near Petoskey Street, Howard Street, and the Bear River mouth.
- Current vesting deed(s) confirming ownership in the City of Petoskey (or other entity).

**2. Recorded boundary surveys and plats**

- Any recorded land surveys, ALTA/NSPS surveys, or survey plats covering Bayfront Park or its component parcels, including any legal descriptions establishing the park's eastern and southern boundaries with Petoskey Street and adjacent streets.
- Any recorded plats for the surrounding subdivision(s) that establish the right-of-way lines bounding the park.

**3. Easements, restrictions, and encumbrances of record**

- Any recorded easements crossing the park parcel(s), including utility easements, drainage easements, access easements, or MNRTF/public grant deed restrictions.
- Any deed restrictions, conservation easements, or reversionary clauses.

**4. Historical ownership chain (brief)**

- Prior vesting deeds sufficient to identify historical use (mill, rail depot, quarry, landfill, or other industrial history) — relevant to a forthcoming Phase I Environmental Site Assessment.
