## Scope of services requested

### Phase I ESA — ASTM E1527 (current edition)

Standard Phase I scope, with the following site-specific emphases:

1. **Records review** — federal and state databases (EDR or equivalent), historical aerial photography, fire insurance maps (Sanborn or equivalent), historical topographic maps, city directory research, and Emmet County deed/title history relevant to industrial or fill activity on or adjacent to the site.

2. **Site reconnaissance** — visual inspection of the bowl depression, visible fill materials, any surface staining, stressed vegetation, or drainage anomalies; inspection of adjacent properties; documentation of any visible debris, metal objects, or unusual fill.

3. **Interviews** — City of Petoskey Parks & DPW staff, historical society contacts as available, any current site users with knowledge of past activities.

4. **Evaluation of RECs**, including:
   - The existing Part 201 listing and its basis (former Petoskey Ford dealership: petroleum, solvents, hydraulic systems)
   - UST registrations/releases on the block and adjoining parcels
   - Neighboring downtown sources (dry cleaners, auto service, USTs)
   - Post-2007 fill or debris placed in the open excavation
   - Status/effect of the 2006 brownfield work and any due-care obligations attaching to the block

5. **Phase I report** per ASTM E1527, including:
   - REC identification and classification (REC / CREC / HREC / de minimis)
   - Recommendations for Phase II investigation, if warranted
   - Identification of any additional investigation needed to support the Michigan Part 201 due-diligence record
