## Request text (paste into the portal description field)

Pursuant to the Michigan Freedom of Information Act, MCL 15.231 et seq., I request copies of
the following records concerning the block bounded by East Lake Street, East Mitchell Street,
Petoskey Street, and US-31 in downtown Petoskey, Emmet County (the former Petoskey
Ford / Petoskey Pointe site; current parcel 52-19-06-227-016):

1. **Part 201 site file.** The complete file for Part 201 site **"Petoskey Ford/Former
   Petoskey Pointe," Site ID 24000048**, including any Phase I or Phase II Environmental
   Site Assessments, Baseline Environmental Assessments, due-care plans or due-care
   compliance analyses, response activity or closure reports, land- or resource-use
   restriction instruments, and associated correspondence.

2. **Storage tank records.** UST/LUST registration, release, corrective-action, and closure
   records for facility **ID 00038614 / 00035252 ("Petoskey Ford Building," 200 E Mitchell
   St)** and any other storage-tank facilities located on the block (former parcels
   52-19-06-227-001 through -016).

3. **Brownfield records.** Work plans, agreements, and reports associated with the 2006
   brownfield award "Petoskey Pointe Project" (NW corner of Mitchell & Petoskey Streets,
   11 parcels).

4. **Excavation-era discharge records.** Any permits, authorizations, or correspondence
   concerning dewatering or stormwater discharge from the 2006–2008 demolition and
   excavation of the block.

5. **Downtown storm outfalls.** NPDES-permitted discharge points or MS4 outfall records for
   the City of Petoskey storm system serving the downtown blocks, at Little Traverse Bay or
   the Bear River.

Please provide records in electronic format via the Request Center. If fees are anticipated
to exceed $50.00, please notify me before proceeding. If any portion of this request is
denied, please cite the specific exemption per MCL 15.240.
