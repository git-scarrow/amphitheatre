## The DR-1 cross (measured — this is the bracket's spine)

Per `dr1_tower_floors/` (commit `388a9fa`), by floor and occluder set:

| by-right floor | eye ft | water band through today's canopy (S2 leaf-on) | if trees removed (S1) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | 635 | **7.7% — blocked** | 100% |
| 3 (top) | 647 | **53.8% — marginal** | 100% |
| *4–8 (NOT by-right)* | 659–707 | *100% regardless* | *100%* |

**Consequence [fact from the two sources above]:** the **by-right building owns no
unconditional bay view**. Its entire view premium is either canopy-contingent
(floors 2–3, trees owned by the City) or entitlement-contingent (floors 4+ require
the PUD/rezoning path the city refused in 2019). The "vertical option" priced by the
~$2.4M strike is therefore substantially an **entitlement option**, and its view
component is controlled by the counterparty's municipal levers on both margins.

## Bracket arithmetic [assumption, swept]

Site 2.01 ac = 87,556 sf. B-2 form: build-to-line, ground-floor commercial required.

| parameter | low | mid | high |
|---|---|---|---|
| plate coverage | 50% (43.8k sf) | 65% (56.9k sf) | 80% (70.0k sf) |
| stories | 3 | 3 | 3 |
| gross sf | 131k | 171k | 210k |
| construction $/sf (mixed-use, N. MI, 2026) | 275 | 325 | 400 |
| construction cost | $36M | $56M | $84M |
| sell/cap value $/sf (blended retail + resi) | 350 | 475 | 650 |
| gross completed value | $46M | $81M | $137M |
| margin before land/soft/L (Part 201) | ~$10M | ~$25M | ~$53M |

Crude and wide by design — the *shape* is the finding, not the point estimate:
by-right economics can pencil at mid/high assumptions **without any view premium**,
because downtown-Petoskey $/sf is scarcity-driven [inference]. The view premium
(canopy- or entitlement-contingent) is upside on top, concentrated at floor 3 lake
side and any floors 4+ ever entitled. `L` (Part 201 remediation) and soft costs are
unpriced and could consume the low-bracket margin entirely [assumption gap — Phase
I/II records request already queued in the design workstream's DATA_GAPS].

## What DR-1 + zoning do to the model's `E(k)` sequencing [inference, evidence line]

De-treeing before site control **gifts the by-right scheme its floor-2/3 water view**
(+53.8 / +23.1 points), raising `V_up` and with it the owner's reservation `R` — at
zero cost to the owner. The same act after site control accrues to the venue's rows
and the neighbors. The DP4 sequencing clause in the adopted addendum already encodes
the gate-side of this; this is the valuation-side mirror, now with measured
magnitudes.
