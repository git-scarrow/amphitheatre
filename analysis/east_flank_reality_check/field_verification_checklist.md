## Field data summary required before design lock

Before any design decision beyond the current budget baseline is locked, the following minimum field data is needed:

1. RTK profiles across the east bowl wall (EF-1, EF-2) — **must be done before extending seating east**
2. Petoskey Street boundary confirmation (PR-1, EF-4) — **must be done before designing Petoskey Street terrace/overlook**
3. Condition photos from all four sides (EF-5, EF-6) — **immediately actionable, no professional required**
4. Slope stability opinion on east rim cuts (GE-3) — **needed before any east-rim grading is specified**

Items 3 and 5 (site photos) can be collected immediately without a survey crew. Items 1, 2, and 4 require field work. Item 4 (property boundary) requires a PLS.
