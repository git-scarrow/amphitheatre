## General language rules for all future documents

1. **East flank rises toward Petoskey Street** (eastward), not toward Lake/Mitchell.
2. **"Budget baseline"** = the 16-row / corner-bays scheme. Not "terrain limit."
3. **"30° escarpment"** — do not use without citing specific multi-scale slope evidence confirming it.
4. **"No retaining walls"** — say "no retaining walls in this design's geometry"; don't extend to a universal terrain claim.
5. **"Gentle east flank"** — replace with "east flank of unverified slope character" or simply "east flank" until forensics confirm character.
6. **"Below the steep band"** — replace with "within the seating band (R85–130 ft), conservatively stopping short of the upper rim."
7. Retaining walls at the east rim should be described as **potential low architectural terrace elements**, not emergency geotechnical interventions.
