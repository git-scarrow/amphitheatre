## 7. Decision Framework

Three resolution paths are available. Each must be explicitly declared:

### Path A ‚ÄĒ Audience-axis alignment (yaw correction)

- Pros: eliminates angular mismatch and lateral offset in a single move.
- Cons: audience looks 26¬į away from bay; the bay+sky backdrop is still visible

### Path B ‚ÄĒ Lateral focal-point shift (preserve bay view, re-centre audience)

to bring the 150¬į axis through the audience centroid.

- Cons: SF moves NE, pushing the stage deeper into the seating fan;
  front-row distance changes; check south-section sightlines from shifted focus.

- Pros: audience-axis mismatch drops to ~11¬į; bay deviation only 15¬į; earthwork minimal.
- Cons: neither metric is fully resolved; requires explicit justification of both residuals.

### Civic/view-axis override (retain 150¬į)
